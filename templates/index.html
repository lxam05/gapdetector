Frequently asked questions

Why not just ask an AI? ▼
Generic AI answers aren't tied to real customers or your actual competitors. Gap Detector pulls from real reviews, forums, and comparison pages — so you get cited evidence, specific gaps, and opportunities you can verify and share with your team. You're building on what people actually said, not on a model's general knowledge.
What does Gap Detector do? ▼
We scan reviews, forums, and comparison pages for any company or product you enter. You get a clear view of where competitors fall short and where customers are frustrated — so you can guide your roadmap and positioning from real feedback instead of guesswork.
How long does a scan take? ▼
Most scans finish within a few minutes. You’ll get a shareable report with gaps, themes, and suggested opportunities. Deeper or broader scans may take a bit longer.
Who is Gap Detector for? ▼
Product, marketing, and founder teams who want to base roadmap and positioning on real customer feedback. It’s especially useful before a launch, when entering a new segment, or when refining your messaging against competitors.
Is there a free trial? ▼
Yes. You can run a free analysis from the homepage. For more scans and full reports, check the Pricing page for plans and credits.
How is this different from manual research? ▼
We aggregate and structure feedback from many sources in one place. Instead of spending days reading reviews and forums yourself, you get a synthesized report with gaps, themes, and opportunities — so you can decide what to build or how to position in hours, not weeks.
